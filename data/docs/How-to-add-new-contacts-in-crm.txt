Add a new contact:

Open the Contacts page: Click the Contacts icon (two people) on the left navigation bar to see your list of leads and customers.

Create a contact: Click the blue Add contact button at the top‑right. A form opens where you can enter details.

Select contact type: Choose the type of contact (Lead, Customer, Supplier, Distributor or CG) from the at the top.

Enter personal details: Fill in the person’s first name, last name, email address, company and branch if applicable.

Add phone numbers: Enter the mobile number with the correct country code. Tick the box if the same number will be used for WhatsApp. If not, enter the WhatsApp number separately.

Complete extra fields: Upload a profile picture and fill any optional fields your organisation uses.

Save the record: Click Add contact at the bottom of the form. The new contact appears in your list.
