To create a marketing template, follow these steps:
1. Click on the "Templates" icon in Jwero.
2. Click on "New Template".
3. Enter the logical title name that reflects the purpose of the template.
4. Select channel (e.g., email, SMS, whatsapp, instagram, messenger).
5. Add content to the template, including variables for personalized messaging.
6. Save and send the template for approval.
