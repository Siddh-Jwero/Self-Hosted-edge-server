Create a segment to group contacts:

Go to Segments: Click the Segments icon (target symbol) on the left to see existing segments.

Start a new segment: Click New segment. Give your segment a name and choose Dynamic or Static.

Set rules: Click Add criteria to select a field (such as Country or Total Purchase Amount), set a condition (equals, greater than, etc.) and enter a value.

Add more conditions: Use Add criteria again and choose AND/OR to combine rules.
