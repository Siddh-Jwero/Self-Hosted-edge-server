To create a utility template, follow these steps:

1. Click on the “Templates” icon in Jwero.
2. Click on “New Template”.
3. Enter the logical title name that reflects the purpose of the template.
4. Select channel (example:- whatsapp,instagram,messenger).
5. Add messsage type
6. Add content to the template, including variables for personalized messaging.
7. Add CTA  and Quick Reply
8. Save and send the template for approval
