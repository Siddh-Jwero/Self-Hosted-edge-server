Edit a contact’s information (detailed):

This is a more detailed version of the earlier guide:

Search for the contact: In the Contacts list, type the contact’s name or email in the search bar. Results update as you type.

Open the profile: Click on the contact’s name. Tabs such as Personal Information, Activities and Notes appear.

Locate the field to edit: Under Personal Information, find the specific field (e.g., mobile number). Next to it is an Edit (pencil) icon—click it.

Enter the new data: Type the updated information. For phone numbers, include the correct dial code.

Save your changes: Click Save or Update. The new information appears immediately.

Repeat for other fields: Continue editing other fields as necessary.
